My Story

My name is Adedokun Abdullahi. I am from Osogbo, Osun State. I have always been interested in technology since I was little. The first item to spark my curiosity was the CD player. I always wondered what "magic" allowed my cartoon to play on the TV after i put a CD in it. I intially studied Pharmacology at Osun State University but left when I realized I wasn't enjoying what I was learning. I searched for a University that was offering Software Engineering while being flexible enough that I could study at my own pace. That was how I found out about Miva Open University.
